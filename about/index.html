Our story

Built in Urbana, run by people who care

All Improvement Cleaning Services was founded by Israel Sanchez right here in Urbana. The name says the goal out loud: every visit should leave your space measurably better, no shortcuts and no excuses.

Like a lot of good local businesses, this one started because the options were not cutting it. Too many cleaning companies quote a great price, then get inconsistent the moment the contract is signed. We wanted to be the company clients could stop worrying about, the one that just handles it.

Today AICS cleans homes and businesses across Urbana, Champaign and the surrounding county. We are small enough that you deal with people who actually know your space, and organized enough to deliver the same standard every single time.

What we stand for

The values behind every clean

Reliability

We show up on schedule and finish the job. Dependability is the whole point of hiring a cleaning company, so we treat it as non-negotiable.

Honesty

Clear quotes, no surprise fees and a straight answer about what a space actually needs. If something can wait, we will tell you.

Care

We treat your home or business like it is our own, from the products we choose to the corners other crews skip.

Local roots

We live and work in Champaign County. Our reputation is built on our neighbors, and that keeps us honest and hungry.

Accountability

If we miss something, tell us within 24 hours and we come back to make it right. No arguments, no runaround.

Communication

Same-day replies, easy scheduling and a real person to talk to. You should never have to chase your cleaning company.
