Post-construction cleaning for a move-in ready space

When the building or remodel is done, a layer of fine dust settles over everything. AICS runs the full post-construction cleanup so the space is clean, safe and ready to hand over, for builders and property owners across Urbana, Champaign, Rantoul and surrounding areas within 50 miles.

Overview

The cleanup that makes a build move-in ready

Construction dust gets into every corner, on every surface and inside every fixture, and a normal clean will not cut it. We clear the debris and the fine dust in phases so the final result is genuinely move-in ready.

We coordinate with contractors and builders around the trades, scheduling each phase so our crew is working when the site is ready and never in the way of the work still going on. That keeps the project moving and the finished space looking the way it should.

The three phases we handle

Post-construction cleanup is not a single visit. It works best in stages, and we scope whichever phases your project needs.

  • Rough cleanRemove large debris and leftover materials, handle general site cleanup, then sweep and vacuum throughout.
  • Interior detail cleanHEPA-vacuum surfaces, wipe down fixtures, clean cabinets inside and out, clean windows, remove paint overspray and adhesive residue and scrub tile.
  • Final and touch-upDetail the floors, polish and sanitize, remove fingerprints from glass, catch last-minute scuffs and polish hardware, typically 24 to 48 hours before handover.

What gets cleaned

By the time we are done, every trace of the build is gone. A full detail covers:

  • Construction dust removed from all surfacesWalls, ledges, trim, vents and light fixtures wiped free of fine dust.
  • Fixtures, cabinets and hardware wiped and polishedInside and out, including handles, pulls and finishes.
  • Windows, frames, sills and tracksGlass cleaned streak-free with tracks and sills cleared of grit.
  • Paint overspray and adhesive residue removedStickers, labels, tape marks and overspray scraped and cleaned away.
  • Floors vacuumed, scrubbed and detailedTile, wood and hard floors cleaned and finished corner to corner.
  • Restrooms and kitchens sanitizedSinks, counters, toilets and appliances disinfected and ready for use.

Residential and commercial

We handle new builds, remodels, tenant improvements and renovations, for both homes and commercial spaces. Once the space is occupied, post-construction cleaning pairs naturally with ongoing commercial cleaning to keep it that way, or with a move-out cleaning style detail when a tenant is turning the space over.

What post-construction cleaning costs

Every site is different, so we quote every job individually based on the square footage, the phase of construction and the finish level. No surprise line items and no obligation, just an honest price before any work begins.

Good questions

Post-construction cleaning FAQs

What is included in post-construction cleaning?

It covers removing construction dust and debris, cleaning fixtures, cabinets, windows, floors and restrooms, and removing paint overspray and adhesive residue.

What are the phases of post-construction cleaning?

There are three phases: a rough clean, an interior detail clean and a final touch-up clean before handover.

How much does post-construction cleaning cost?

It depends on the size of the space, the phase of construction and the finish level, so we give you an exact, no-obligation quote after a walkthrough. Higher-finish and medical spaces take more detail work, and your quote will reflect that before any work begins.

When should the final cleaning happen?

Usually 24 to 48 hours before handover or move-in so the space stays spotless for the walkthrough.

Do you work with contractors and builders?

Yes, we coordinate around the trades and schedule each phase to fit the build timeline.

Do you handle both residential and commercial construction cleanup?

Yes, we handle new builds, remodels and tenant improvements for homes and businesses.
