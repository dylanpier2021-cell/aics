Overview

A cleaner home on a schedule that fits real life

Life gets full. Between work, family and everything else, the cleaning is usually the thing that slips. AICS cleans houses, apartments and condos on a schedule that fits how you actually live, whether that is a weekly reset, a biweekly refresh or a monthly once-over. You get the same trusted team and the same consistent results every visit.

We serve homes throughout Urbana, Champaign, Savoy, Mahomet, St. Joseph and Rantoul, along with the surrounding areas within 50 miles. Tell us how you like your home kept and we build the visit around your priorities, so the details that matter to you never get missed.

What a standard clean covers

Every home is a little different, but a typical standard visit includes:

  • KitchenCounters, sink, stovetop, the outside of appliances and floors cleaned and wiped down.
  • BathroomsToilets, tubs and showers, sinks and mirrors cleaned, with floors disinfected.
  • Bedrooms and living areasDusting, surfaces wiped, a general tidy up and beds made on request.
  • Floors throughoutVacuumed and mopped across the home, room to room.
  • Trash emptied and general tidyBins emptied and relined, clutter reset so every room feels put together.
  • Consistent checklist every visitThe same thorough routine each time, so nothing gets skipped or forgotten.

Standard clean vs deep clean

A standard clean is maintenance. It keeps an already tidy home fresh from one visit to the next. A deep clean goes further and takes on the buildup that a routine clean does not reach: baseboards, inside the oven and fridge on request, interior windows, grout, vents, light fixtures and the spots behind and under furniture.

Most clients start with a one-time deep clean to bring the whole home up to a great baseline, then keep it there with recurring standard visits. If you are wrapping up a lease or getting a place ready for the next resident, ask about our move-out cleaning, and if you want the glass to sparkle inside and out, we also offer window cleaning.

Good questions

House cleaning FAQs

How much does house cleaning cost?

Your price depends mostly on the size and condition of your home and how often you want service, so a recurring maintenance clean and a one-time deep clean are quoted differently. We give you a clear, free quote before we ever start, with no surprise line items and no obligation.

What is the difference between a standard clean and a deep clean?

A standard clean is regular maintenance that keeps an already tidy home fresh: kitchens, bathrooms, dusting, floors and general upkeep. A deep clean tackles the buildup that a routine clean does not reach, such as baseboards, inside appliances, grout, window tracks and behind furniture. Most homes start with a deep clean, then stay fresh with standard visits.

How often should I schedule house cleaning?

Busy homes with kids, pets or a full schedule usually do best on weekly or biweekly service. Lighter or smaller homes often stay comfortable with a monthly visit plus a deep clean a few times a year. We are glad to help you pick a rhythm that keeps your home the way you like it without paying for more than you need.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No. Most of our regular clients give us access through a key, door code or lockbox and simply come home to a clean house. You are welcome to be there, especially for the first visit so we can walk through your priorities, but it is not required.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, we arrive with professional cleaning products and equipment, so you do not need to have anything on hand. If you prefer specific products for allergies, pets or a particular surface, we are happy to use what you provide.

Is your team insured and trustworthy?

Yes. AICS is a licensed Illinois LLC and carries liability insurance. You get the same consistent, respectful team in your home, so you always know who is cleaning and can trust the results visit after visit.
