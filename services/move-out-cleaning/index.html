Move-out cleaning that helps you get your deposit back

Moving is stressful enough without scrubbing the place you are leaving. AICS handles the detailed top-to-bottom clean landlords look for, plus move-in cleans for the place you are heading to, across Urbana, Champaign and Champaign County.

Overview

A top-to-bottom clean built around the walkthrough

At move-out, the property gets inspected closely, and small misses can cost you the deposit. We clean to the checklist that landlords and property managers actually use, so the place holds up when someone walks through with a clipboard.

It is a great fit for renters who want their full deposit back, home sellers who need to show a spotless property, and landlords turning a unit between tenants.

What a move-out clean covers

Every home is a little different, but a standard move-out clean covers:

  • Floors swept, vacuumed and mopped throughoutCarpet, tile and hard floors cleaned in every room, including the corners that get skipped.
  • Bathrooms scrubbed and disinfected top to bottomTubs, showers, toilets, sinks, mirrors and tile fully sanitized.
  • Kitchen counters, sinks and backsplash cleanedEvery surface degreased and wiped so the kitchen looks move-in ready.
  • Cabinets and drawers wiped inside and outCrumbs and dust cleared from every shelf, drawer and cabinet face.
  • Appliances cleaned inside and out (fridge, oven, microwave)Detailed inside and out, not just a quick pass over the fronts.
  • Baseboards, blinds, light fixtures and switch platesThe dusty details inspectors look for, wiped down throughout.
  • Closets, shelves and interior windowsEmptied closets, shelving and interior glass left clean and clear.
  • Trash removed and bins wipedLeftover trash hauled out and bins rinsed so nothing lingers.

Add-ons and what is not included

We like to be honest about scope so nothing gets padded onto your quote. Carpet shampooing and wall repair or painting are not part of a standard move-out clean. What is included are the appliance interiors, the fridge, oven and microwave, cleaned as part of the base service. If you do want the carpets deep cleaned or the interior windows detailed, we can add those on, just let us know when you book.

Move-in cleaning too

You do not want to unpack into someone else's dust. We can clean the new place before you move a single box in, so your fresh start is actually fresh. It pairs well with an ongoing home cleaning plan once you are settled, and a round of window cleaning to let the light in.

Good questions

Move-out cleaning FAQs

How much does move-out cleaning cost?

Most move-out cleans run from about 110 to 650 dollars, with a national average around 360 dollars. Where you land in that range comes down to the size of the home and the condition it is in. We give you an exact price after a quick look at the space.

What is included in a move-out clean?

A standard move-out clean covers floors, bathrooms and the kitchen, the inside and outside of cabinets and drawers, appliance interiors, baseboards and fixtures, and closets and interior windows. In short, the whole place gets a detailed top-to-bottom pass.

Will this help me get my deposit back?

We clean to the same checklist landlords inspect against, which gives you the best possible shot at your full deposit. The final decision is always the landlord's, but a spotless unit removes the easy reasons to withhold any of it.

What is not included?

Carpet shampooing and wall repair or painting are not part of a standard move-out clean. If you do want the carpets deep cleaned, we can add that on, just ask when you book.

Do you offer move-in cleaning too?

Yes. We are happy to clean the new place before you move in, so you unpack into a fresh, spotless home instead of someone else's dust.

How far in advance should I book?

As early as you can, especially around the end of the month when moves pile up and the calendar fills fast. A day or two before your final walkthrough works best, so the place stays clean until you hand over the keys.

Ready to hand over a spotless place?

Book your move-out or move-in clean and give yourself the best shot at a full deposit or a fresh start in a home that is truly clean.
