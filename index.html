Good questions

Cleaning service FAQs

The things people ask us most, answered straight. Do not see yours? Send it over.

How much do professional cleaning services cost in Urbana and Champaign?

Every job is priced on its own because square footage, condition and how often you want service all move the number. We do not post set prices, because a fair number depends on your specific space, so instead we give free, no-pressure quotes, often the same day, and you know the exact price before we start.

Do I need to be home while you clean?

No. Most of our clients give us access through a key, a garage or door code, or a lockbox and go about their day. If you would rather be there for the first visit to walk us through your priorities, that is welcome too. Whatever you are comfortable with works for us.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes. Our team arrives with professional-grade products, vacuums, mops and all the tools needed for the job. If you prefer we use specific products for allergies, pets or a sensitive surface, just let us know and we will use what you provide.

Is All Improvement Cleaning Services insured?

Yes. We are a licensed Illinois LLC and carry liability insurance, so your home or business is protected while we work. We are happy to share proof of coverage before a commercial contract begins.

How often should I schedule cleaning?

It depends on the space and how it is used. Busy households and offices often do best with weekly or every-other-week service, while lighter-use spaces are fine with monthly visits plus a deep clean two to four times a year. We will recommend a schedule that keeps things looking their best without paying for more than you need.

What is the difference between a standard clean and a deep clean?

A standard clean is maintenance: dusting, floors, kitchens and bathrooms wiped down and refreshed. A deep clean goes after the buildup a routine visit skips, such as baseboards, inside appliances, grout, window tracks and behind furniture. Most people start with a one-time deep clean, then keep it up with standard visits.

Do you handle both residential and commercial cleaning?

We do. AICS cleans houses and apartments as well as offices, retail spaces, medical suites, schools and newly built or renovated properties. Because one local team handles both, it is easy to bundle services like a home clean plus windows, or an office clean plus floor waxing.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Urbana and regularly clean in Champaign, Rantoul, Savoy, Mahomet, St. Joseph and the surrounding towns, and we travel up to about 50 miles from Urbana. That reaches well past Champaign County, so if you are not sure whether you are in range, just ask and we will give you a straight answer.
